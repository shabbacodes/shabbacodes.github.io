From August 2018 to July 2019, I served the federal republic of Nigeria under the National Youth Service (NYSC) Scheme. Under this scheme, I served as the Vice President of the Charity Community Development Service Group. During my tenure, I organized and scheduled a number of events that included educating & mentoring and donating goods to underprivileged children.

Upon returning to Nigeria in August 2018, I decided to return to my previous middle school to give a career and wellness advise talk

Between May and July 2019, I joined Mould Africa. In a bid to engage more Nigerian youths, I joined this organization to mentor high school children in Kings College Nigeria. During this 6-week program, I mentored a small classroom of boys by engaging them with stories that held important life changing lessons.
